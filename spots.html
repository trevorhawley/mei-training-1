Favorite Spots

Where cats actually sleep — ranked by frequency, weirdness, and the level of inconvenience caused.

We surveyed cat owners about the strangest places their cats sleep. The results were not surprising. They were, however, very specific.

Rank 01 · Most common

The cardboard box

Reported by 94% of owners. The box doesn't need to be comfortable. It doesn't need to be large. It needs to be a box.

Why

Enclosed sides = security. Cardboard = warmth retention. The cat is not wrong.

Rank 02

The keyboard

Warm, flat, and directly between you and the thing you're paying attention to. From the cat's view, this is a feature.

Why

Warmth from the laptop below, attention from the human above. Two needs, one rectangle.

Rank 03

The bathroom sink

A summer favorite. Cool porcelain, curved sides, occasional fresh water. The cat considers this a spa.

Why

Temperature regulation. Cats overheat easily; porcelain stays cool to the touch.

Rank 04

Inside your shoe

Not next to your shoe. Inside it. Curled into a shoe like it's a sleeping bag made of you.

Why

Your scent. Cats find their humans' smell calming. The shoe is, in a way, a hug.

Rank 05 · Most precarious

The vase

High, narrow, breakable, and entirely unsuitable. The cat disagrees. The cat has always disagreed.

Why

Elevation = vantage point. Tight fit = security. The danger is, apparently, part of the appeal.

Rank 06

The tote bag

Open for thirty seconds. Occupied. The handles form a convenient headrest.

Why

Soft fabric, enclosed sides, smells like wherever you just were. A portable den.

Rank 07 · Most architectural

The bookshelf gap

Books will be removed. A gap will be made. The gap will be slept in. The books will not be returned.

Why

Elevation plus enclosure. The cat has effectively built custom furniture using your library.

Rank 08 · Honorable mention

On top of you

Not a space, exactly. A person. Specifically, your chest, at 3 AM, with full weight and zero warning.

Why

Heartbeat, warmth, and the certainty that you are not going anywhere. The highest compliment.
