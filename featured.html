The story

How Mochi found the linen closet

Mochi's person, Yuki, had been looking for her for an entire afternoon. The treats were shaken. The crinkle bag was crinkled. Nothing. The search expanded to the garden, then the street, then increasingly desperate phone calls.

At 3:47 PM, a soft thump came from the hallway closet. The door had been left slightly ajar. Mochi had climbed to the third shelf, made a nest in the spare towels, and was deeply, contentedly asleep — entirely unbothered by the chaos she'd caused.

She has returned to that shelf every afternoon since. The door now has a small cat-flap cut into the bottom. The towels have been formally reassigned.

"She didn't choose the shelf. The shelf chose her."
— Yuki, Mochi's person
