About us

We just live with cats

No certifications. No credentials. Just twenty years of sharing a house with animals who sleep in our shoes.

How this started

A shoe, a photo, a website

In 2004, one of us got a cat. Then another. Then, somehow, several more. Twenty years later, there are always cats in the house — usually asleep, often somewhere inconvenient, occasionally in a place that made us laugh hard enough to grab a camera.

Nozzle is the result. It's not a business. It's not a portfolio. It's a slow, ongoing record of the weird places our cats have chosen to sleep, and a small attempt to explain why they do it.

We're not certified cat photographers. We're not certified anything. We're two people who have lived with cats long enough to know that the box is always going to win.

What we believe

A few small principles

  • The cat is not wrong. If they're sleeping somewhere strange, there's a reason. It's usually a good one.
  • Don't wake a sleeping cat for a photo. Every picture on this site was taken without disturbing the subject. If we missed the shot, we missed the shot.
  • The box always wins. You can buy the fancy bed. The cat will sleep in the box the fancy bed came in.
  • Slow blinks are a two-way street. Return them. Always.
